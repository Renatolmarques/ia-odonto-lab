# Clinic Knowledge Base — Smile Studio Dental Clinic
# Sample data for demonstration purposes — not real clinic data

## 1. Services and Treatments

- **Teeth Whitening**: Aesthetic procedure. Session lasts 1h30. Avoid staining foods for 48h. Results last 12–18 months.
- **Dental Implants**: Permanent tooth replacement surgery (~1h). 24–48h rest recommended. Success rate above 95%.
- **Porcelain Veneers**: Cosmetic facets applied over existing teeth. Requires 2–3 appointments.
- **Orthodontics**: Traditional braces and Invisalign. Maintenance appointments average 45 minutes.
- **Prophylaxis (Cleaning)**: Full professional cleaning lasting 1h. Recommended every 6 months.
- **Root Canal**: Endodontic treatment to save compromised teeth. 1–2 sessions depending on complexity.

## 4. Pricing Policy

- **Initial Evaluation**: Complimentary — no charge for the first consultation.
- **Payment**: PIX, debit, credit card (up to 12 interest-free installments).
- **Insurance**: Private pay only.
- **Pricing note**: Implants, veneers, and orthodontics do not have fixed prices.
  Exact cost depends on clinical evaluation and personalized treatment planning.

## 5. Clinical Protocols

- All patients complete a health questionnaire before treatment.
- Phobias and allergies are recorded at the first visit and updated at every return.
- Patients with dental anxiety receive special care: topical anesthesia before injections,
  calming music, and the option to pause during procedures.
- Post-procedure instructions are sent via WhatsApp after every appointment.
